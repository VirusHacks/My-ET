# Product Specification: The AI-Native News OS

## Core Vision
Transition ET from a static newspaper to a personalized "News Operating System" that adapts content, format, and depth to each individual user's persona and context.

## Persona-Driven Features
### 1. Finance & Investors
- **Bull/Bear Splitter:** Dual-agent analysis highlighting the 3 strongest growth signals vs. 3 deepest risks.
- **Watchlist Cross-Referencing:** Secure local-data (via MCP) cross-referenced with global macro news for instant portfolio impact alerts.

### 2. Legal & Policy Sectors
- **Clause Extractor:** Real-time redlining of new regulations, translating legalese into plain English business impacts.

### 3. Startup Founders & Executives
- **Competitor Radar:** AI-driven archival search to report on how competitors responded to similar market shifts.
- **Executive Memo:** One-click conversion of 1,000-word editorials into a strict 3-part strategic memo (Event, Threat, Action).

### 4. Students & Academics
- **Flashcard & Quiz Generator:** Converting complex economic opinion pieces into educational tools for UPSC/MBA preparation.

## Multimodal "News Studio" Concept
- **AI Podcast:** Two-person dialogue explaining article implications for the commute.
- **AI Video Studio:** 60-second TikTok/short-form video generation with visual cues.
- **Story Arc Tracker:** Interactive, visual timelines tracing a story's evolution and sentiment shifts.

## System-Level Innovations
- **Reddit-Style AI Builder:** Dynamic article generation from user prompts ("How does X affect Y?").
- **Cultural Vernacular Engine:** Translation that swaps metaphors (e.g., Wall Street idioms) with local analogies (e.g., regional trade idioms).
- **Misinformation Engine:** Vision-based fact-checking for WhatsApp screenshots and social media links.
- **The Contrarian Toggle (Bias Breaker):** Building ultimate trust by viewing the exact opposite argument for any opinion piece.
