# Feature Guide: The AI-Native News OS

## Core Vision
To revolutionize how high-stakes business and financial information is consumed by transforming a static newspaper into a **personalized, autonomous, and multimodal News Operating System.**

---

## 1. News Navigator (The Custom Newsroom)
Instead of scrolling a fixed page, users interact with a **dynamic search-first interface.**
- **Autonomous Article Builder**: Powered by **Tavily Search** and **ET Archives**, the system can synthesize a custom briefing from scratch based on a user prompt (e.g., *"How does the current semiconductor shortage affect Indian auto-dealers?"*).
- **Interactive Deep-Dive**: Once a custom article is generated, users can chat with it to ask follow-up questions, drill down into data, or verify sources.
- **Strategic Impact**: Moves news from a "passive broadcast" to an "active intelligence tool."

---

## 2. ET "News Studio" (Multimodal Transformations)
Every article acts as a base for multiple AI-generated formats, tailored to the user's focus.

### Multimodal Deliverables
- **AI Audio Briefings (Podcasts)**: A conversational 2-person dialogue explaining the news for the commute.
- **AI Video Studio**: Auto-generated 60-second broadcast or TikTok-style news scripts with visual narrative cues.
- **Interactive Timelines (Story Arc)**: A visual representation of a story's chronology, mapping key players, sentiment shifts, and "what-if" predictions.

### Sector-Specific Studio Actions
- **Bull/Bear Splitter (Finance)**: A multi-agent "debate" that ruthlessly analyzes an article to generate 3 bullish growth signals and 3 bearish risks.
- **Regulatory Redliner (Law/Policy)**: Automatically extracts legal clauses or SEBI policy changes, translating them into "plain English" business impacts.
- **Competitor Radar (Founders)**: Leverages ET's archives to show exactly how competitors (e.g., Blinkit vs Zepto) have historically responded to similar market shifts.
- **Retention Flashcards (Students)**: Instantly generates UPSC/MBA-ready flashcards and quizzes from complex editorials.

---

## 3. The Truth Engine (Misinformation Engine)
A high-authority portal to dissolve fake financial news.
- **Vision-Based Debunking**: Users upload WhatsApp screenshots or paste X (Twitter) links.
- **Verified Fact-Checking**: The system cross-references claims against ET's verified reporting archive and official government filings using **Multimodal Reasoning models (Gemini Pro Vision)** and **Tavily Search.**
- **Verdict Scoring**: Outputs a definitive "True, False, or Misleading" score with clickable, verified citations.

---

## 4. Vernacular Business News Engine
Built for the "Next Billion" users through high-fidelity regional context.
- **Cultural Translation**: Powered by **Sarvam AI**, the system swaps global/Western metaphors (e.g., "Wall Street Bear Hug") for culturally relevant local analogies, making complex high-finance accessible to everyone.
- **Dialect Adaptation**: Uses regional location data to adjust the tone and idiom of the translation, rather than providing a literal, word-for-word translation.

---

## 5. System Personalization & Memory
- **The "Memory Layer" (Mem0/Redis)**: The system remembers your watchlist, your past searches, and your professional sector across sessions. This allows the AI to provide **"Proactive Insights"** (e.g., *"Wait, you're reading about semiconductor shortages—did you know this directly affects Tata Motors in your portfolio?"*).
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**: Securely connects to your private local data (bank CSVs, local portfolios) to provide ultra-personalized financial advice without sending the raw private data to external AI servers.
